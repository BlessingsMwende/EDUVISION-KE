# EduVision-KE  
## National AI System for Human Capital Intelligence  

EduVision-KE is a national-grade education intelligence infrastructure designed to transform learner data into actionable insights for governance, equity, and sustainable development.

---

##  Architecture Layers

### 1️⃣ National Backbone
- Counties
- Schools
- Learners
- Enrollments

### 2️⃣ Security & Governance
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- User Management
- Audit Logging

### 3️⃣ Intelligence Foundation
- Attendance tracking
- Academic assessments

### 4️⃣ AI Intelligence Layer
- Performance prediction (Exceeding / Meeting / Below)
- STEM readiness scoring
- Risk signal detection

### 5️⃣ Integrity & Anti-Manipulation Engine
- Ghost learner detection
- Duplicate enrollment monitoring
- Suspicious pattern flagging

---

##  National Objectives

- Strengthen education data integrity  
- Enable early learner intervention  
- Support STEM pipeline planning  
- Improve transparency and governance  
- Provide evidence-based national planning
