## 1. Problem (20 seconds)
Many learners in Kenya fall behind or drop out because risks are identified too late, while talents beyond exams remain undiscovered. Current systems are reactive, fragmented, and exam-focused.

## 2. Solution (30 seconds)
EDUVISION-KE uses AI to analyze learner performance trends, attendance, and contextual signals to:
- predict early risk,
- discover talents aligned with CBC competencies,
- support inclusive and data-driven decision-making.

## 3. Live Demo Flow (50 seconds)
1. Open the EDUVISION-KE dashboard.
2. Upload a sample learner dataset.
3. The system generates:
   - a risk score (Low / Medium / High),
   - identified learner strengths,
   - a brief explanation of key factors.
4. Decision recommendations are shown for teachers and parents.

## 4. Impact (20 seconds)
- Early intervention before dropout.
- Fair talent identification beyond exams.
- Better resource allocation for schools and policymakers.

## 5. Closing (20 seconds)
EDUVISION-KE demonstrates how responsible AI can transform education outcomes while remaining secure, inclusive, and aligned with national policy.
